It will Setup Your SAMBA Share Drive

- SAMBA is a free, open-source software suite that builds a bridge for Windows & Mac machines to easily access directories hosted on a Linux server (often used for Network Attached Storage, or NAS).
- For first-time Linux users this process can be confusing & intimidating. You just want cloud storage at home dagnabbit! Why all this typing and code, no icons to click on?!
- SamWizard is here to build that bridge with you, guiding you with a familiar interface.

Setting up TailScale access

- Normally, SAMBA only works if your devices are plugged into the exact same home or office router.
- Without Tailscale, accessing a SAMBA server from outside your house is incredibly dangerous. It requires opening holes in your home router (port forwarding), which invites hackers to attack your server.
- Tailscale creates a secure, private network (your 'Tailnet') allowing only devices YOU designate to access your server from anywhere.
